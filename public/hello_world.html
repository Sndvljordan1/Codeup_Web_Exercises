My Dream Vehicle

My dream vehicle would have to be the PAL-V. The PAL-V is considered a "flying car", although its shape is more akin to that of an enclosed motorcycle. The "car" can seat two and can "transform" into a gyrocoptor capable of flight. One unique aspect of this vehicle is that it should be flown below commercial airspace, which allows for flights with no need for future planning/coordination with air traffic controlls.
